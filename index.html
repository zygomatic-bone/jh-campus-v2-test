About JSA

A peaceful campus, a serious curriculum.

Associated with the historic Jamia Masjid Bangalore, the Academy opens up school and higher-education pathways for 7th+ and 10+2 class students, Hafiz-e-Quran, and Aalim students.

Two disciplines, one daily rhythm

The Academy provides a well-furnished, peaceful learning environment under experienced teachers and instructors, running a comprehensive academic program fully compliant with NIOS, SSLC, PUC Karnataka, and Bangalore University curricula.

Alongside academic coaching, daily memorization of Quran e Majeed is compulsory for every Hafiz-e-Quran student — discipline and devotion built into the same timetable as exam preparation.

The focus extends beyond marks: building literacy and numeracy for students who need extra support, and shaping every student into a responsible citizen of India.

Rector's Message

Educating for Deen & Duniya

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

My warmest greetings and welcomes to all our students, parents, educators, and well-wishers of the Jamia Shaheen family.

Real empowerment begins when we reject ignorance with comprehensive knowledge and embrace the world with unity. In today's competitive and rapidly evolving environment, education must go beyond textbooks and examinations. It must cultivate wisdom, character, leadership, and responsibility.

At Jamia Shaheen, our foundational vision is to bridge the gap between timeless moral values and contemporary academic excellence. We strive to create a learning ecosystem where the principles of Deen and the opportunities of Duniya coexist harmoniously, enabling students to excel in both their spiritual and worldly pursuits.

Our mission extends beyond producing academically successful individuals. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds who possess integrity, compassion, intellectual curiosity, and a deep sense of social responsibility. Through a balanced blend of Islamic values and modern education, we aim to prepare future leaders who will contribute positively to their communities, their nation, and humanity at large.

We firmly believe that true success is achieved when knowledge is accompanied by strong character. Therefore, we encourage every student to pursue excellence (Ihsan) in all aspects of life while embracing patience (Sabr), discipline, and perseverance in the face of challenges.

Jamia Shaheen remains committed to providing a secure, supportive, and inspiring environment where students can realize their full potential while staying deeply connected to their faith, culture, and values.

As we move forward together, let us continue to seek knowledge, uphold noble character, and dedicate our efforts toward meaningful service. May our achievements become a source of benefit for society and a means of earning the pleasure of Almighty Allah.

May Almighty Allah bless our collective intentions, guide our endeavors, and grant success to every member of the Jamia Shaheen family.

Wassalamu Alaikum Wa Rahmatullahi Wa Barakatuh.

Hazrath Moulana Mufti Dr. Mohamed Maqsood Imran Rashadi
Rector, Jamia Shaheen
